
# AI Impact on Student Life — Power BI Dashboard

## Overview
Analysis of how AI tools are impacting academic performance, 
time management, and career confidence across 1,500 students 
in 2026. The analysis explores GPA changes, tool effectiveness, 
usage patterns, and the relationship between AI ethics concerns 
and career outlook.

## Key Findings
- 59.6% of students improved their GPA after adopting AI tools
- Average GPA improvement of +0.088 across all majors
- Software Engineering students benefit most (+0.102 GPA change)
- Fine Arts students benefit least (+0.075 GPA change)
- AI saves students an average of 8.5 hours per week 
  regardless of which tool they use
- All 5 AI tools perform similarly — tool choice matters 
  less than how you use it
- Essay Drafting saves the most weekly hours across all usage types
- 20% of students saw GPA decline despite using AI — 
  suggesting over-reliance can hurt performance
- AI ethics concerns have no impact on career confidence — 
  High, Medium, and Low concern groups show similar scores

## Tools Used
Power BI, Excel, DAX measures, Calculated columns

## Dataset
Synthetic dataset — AI Impact on Student Life 2026
1,500 student records across 6 majors and 5 AI tools
